How it works

Three steps, no detours

Copy a share link from the Pinterest app or site, drop it in, and grab your file.

01

Copy the share link

Open the pin, tap the ••• menu, and choose "Copy link" (or "Share to" on desktop).

02

Paste it above

Drop the link into the box and hit Fetch download links. We'll pull the available qualities.

03

Pick a quality & save

Choose a resolution and tap Download file. It lands straight in your downloads folder.
